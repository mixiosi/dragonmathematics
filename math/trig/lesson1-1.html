Angles and Their Measurement

Core Concepts

Classification of Angles

Acute Angle

\(0^\circ < \theta < 90^\circ\)

Right Angle

\(\theta = 90^\circ\)

Obtuse Angle

\(90^\circ < \theta < 180^\circ\)

Angular Measurement Systems

Degree System

  • \(1^\circ = 60'\) (minutes)
  • \(1' = 60''\) (seconds)
  • Full circle: \(360^\circ\)

Radian System

  • \(1 \text{ rad} = \frac{180^\circ}{\pi}\)
  • Full circle: \(2\pi \text{ rad}\)
  • \( \pi \text{ rad} = 180^\circ\)
Key Conversion Formulas:
\( \text{Radians} = \text{Degrees} \times \frac{\pi}{180} \)
\( \text{Degrees} = \text{Radians} \times \frac{180}{\pi} \)

Conversion Example

Convert \(135^\circ\) to radians:

\[ 135^\circ \times \frac{\pi}{180} = \frac{3\pi}{4} \text{ radians} \]

Verify:
\(\frac{3\pi}{4} \times \frac{180}{\pi} = 135^\circ\)
