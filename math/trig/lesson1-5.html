The Unit Circle: Foundation of Trigonometry

Unlock the power of circular trigonometry and periodic functions

Fundamental Equation

The unit circle equation:

\[ x^2 + y^2 = 1 \]

Coordinates correspond to:

\[ (x, y) = (\cos\theta, \sin\theta) \]

Periodicity of Functions

Sine and Cosine:

\[ \sin(\theta + 2\pi) = \sin\theta \] \[ \cos(\theta + 2\pi) = \cos\theta \]

Tangent:

\[ \tan(\theta + \pi) = \tan\theta \]
