Graphing Reciprocal Trigonometric Functions

Master asymptotes, periodicity, and behavior of tan, cot, and csc

Core Function Properties

Tangent
  • Period: \( \pi \)
  • Asymptotes: \( \frac{\pi}{2} + k\pi \)
  • Range: \( (-\infty, \infty) \)
Cotangent
  • Period: \( \pi \)
  • Asymptotes: \( k\pi \)
  • Range: \( (-\infty, \infty) \)
Cosecant
  • Period: \( 2\pi \)
  • Asymptotes: \( k\pi \)
  • Range: \( (-\infty, -1] \cup [1, \infty) \)

Comparative Analysis

Function Period Asymptotes Range
\( y = \tan(x) \) \( \pi \) \( \frac{\pi}{2} + k\pi \) All real numbers
\( y = \cot(x) \) \( \pi \) \( k\pi \) All real numbers
\( y = \csc(x) \) \( 2\pi \) \( k\pi \) \( |y| \geq 1 \)

Solving Equations Graphically

Example 1: \( \tan(x) = 1 \)

Solutions occur at:

\[ x = \frac{\pi}{4} + k\pi,\ k \in \mathbb{Z} \]
Example 2: \( \csc(x) = 2 \)

Solutions occur at:

\[ x = \frac{\pi}{6} + 2k\pi\ \text{and}\ \frac{5\pi}{6} + 2k\pi,\ k \in \mathbb{Z} \]
