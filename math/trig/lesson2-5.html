Sum & Difference Formulas

Master angle addition and subtraction relationships

Core Angle Formulas

Sine Formulas

\[ \sin(A + B) = \sin A \cos B + \cos A \sin B \]
\[ \sin(A - B) = \sin A \cos B - \cos A \sin B \]

Cosine Formulas

\[ \cos(A + B) = \cos A \cos B - \sin A \sin B \]
\[ \cos(A - B) = \cos A \cos B + \sin A \sin B \]

Tangent Formulas

\[ \tan(A + B) = \frac{\tan A + \tan B}{1 - \tan A \tan B} \]
\[ \tan(A - B) = \frac{\tan A - \tan B}{1 + \tan A \tan B} \]

Worked Example

Find \(\cos(15^\circ)\) using difference formula

  1. Express as difference: \[ 15^\circ = 45^\circ - 30^\circ \]
  2. Apply cosine difference formula: \[ \cos(45^\circ - 30^\circ) = \cos45^\circ\cos30^\circ + \sin45^\circ\sin30^\circ \]
  3. Calculate values: \[ = \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2} \cdot \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2} + \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2} \cdot \frac{1}{2} = \frac{\sqrt{6} + \sqrt{2}}{4} \]
